🤖 AI Journal Recommendation System

How it works:

  • Keyword Matching: Extracts keywords from your abstract and matches them with journal name, field, and publisher.
  • Match Score: Each keyword match increases the relevance score.
  • Ranking: Journals are ranked based on the highest match score.

Acceptance Probability is estimated using:

  • 📊 Match Score (60%)
  • 🏆 Journal Rank (30%)
  • ✍️ Abstract Quality (10%)

Color Indicators:

  • 🟢 High probability (>70%)
  • 🟠 Medium probability (40–70%)
  • 🔴 Low probability (<40%)

Note: This is an AI-assisted estimation. It does not guarantee acceptance.
